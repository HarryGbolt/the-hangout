Who we are

A Space That's Yours

The Hangout is a free, safe space for young people aged 11–17 to come together, meet new friends, have fun and just be themselves.

Every Thursday evening at Caister Community Spoon, we bring together young people from the local area for a relaxed, welcoming session full of activities, games and great vibes.
